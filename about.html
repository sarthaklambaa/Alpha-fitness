The Gym was started by two brothers, whose fitness Journey began way back in their 20s. They have been highly motivated to get their body fit and it inspired them to help other people achieve their fitness goals. They inspired thousands of people to improve and change their unhealthy lifestyles to healthy and Disciplined Lifestyles. Till now Alpha Fitness has served 10,000+ Clients in the northwest region of New Delhi by providing them Diet Plans and exercise charts under the guidance of certified nutritionists and Trainers. Workout with a purpose. Work out to help you live the life you want to live. Work out to support your goals, whatever they may be.

Customer Satisfaction is Our Primary Goal We know that building a positive culture is incredibly important. We believe in encouraging, supporting, challenging, learning, and growing to be the best! At Alpha Fitness you’ll find all the latest strength and cardio equipment along with an energetic group exercise program that includes Zumba, Kickboxing, Bootcamp, Muscle Building, and many other cardio classes. You’ll find a supportive environment with all kinds of people who are working just as hard as you to meet their goals.

We are a group of certified professional trainers whose vision is to provide Fitness Consultancy, Diet Plans, and Nutrition Goals and help people to achieve their goals.Our only Motive is to "Train like a Beast and look like a Beauty".
Getting to a healthier place is about more than going to the gym. That’s why we offer personal training and classes when you’re here, and the right tools to keep you on track when you’re not.
